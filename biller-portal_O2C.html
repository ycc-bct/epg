Bill Status Reference
Valid
Bill passed all validation checks and is ready for submission.
Fully Paid
This bill has already been paid in full. It will be excluded from the batch submission.
Hold
Bill is flagged by the FE (e.g. dispute, duplicate, or account issue). Contact ePG support to resolve.
Error
Validation failed (e.g. missing field, invalid biller code). Correct the record and re-upload.
